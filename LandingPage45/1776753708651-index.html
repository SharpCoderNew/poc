The Atmosphere of Emotion

Blending ethereal synthesizers with raw, intimate vocals, Sofia Kline has carved a unique space in the modern electronic landscape. Her music explores the intersections of memory, technology, and the natural world.

Born in London and now based in the creative heart of Berlin, Sofia began her journey as a classical pianist before finding her voice in the glitchy textures of experimental electronica.
