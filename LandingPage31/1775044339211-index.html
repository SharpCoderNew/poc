Our Philosophy

Benvenuti to AperiBar

Inspired by the classic bars of Milan and Rome, AperiBar brings the essential Italian ritual of Aperitivo to New York City. We believe in the beauty of a well-mixed Negroni, the crunch of a fresh focaccia, and the lively conversation that bridges the gap between work and dinner.

Beverage Program

From the Spritz Veneziano to our Signature Smoked Old Fashioned, our menu celebrates the diversity of Italian spirits.

Kitchen Highlights

House-made pastas, seasonal cicchetti, and imported cheeses curated to pair perfectly with your drink of choice.
