Visions in Violet

Based in Berlin, Sofia Kline blends cinematic electronica with deeply personal storytelling. Her soundscapes are woven from modular synths, ethereal vocal layers, and the rhythmic heartbeat of the city at night.

Following the success of her debut EP 'Neon Silence', Sofia has become a staple of the underground electronic scene, known for her immersive live performances that blur the line between concert and installation art.
