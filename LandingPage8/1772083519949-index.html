The Spirit of Aperitivo

Inspired by the classic Italian tradition of transitioning from work to play, AperiBar brings the soul of Milan to Midtown Manhattan. From sunrise espresso and artisanal pastries to late-night negronis and wood-fired pizzas, we offer a sophisticated yet relaxed sanctuary for travelers and locals alike.
