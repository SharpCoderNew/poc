About HackIndia 2026

Where Silicon Valley meets the silicon coast of Bangalore.

HackIndia is more than just a competition; it's a movement to empower the next wave of Indian developers. In 2026, we are zeroing in on the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Web3 technology.

Participants will have 48 hours to design, develop, and pitch solutions that leverage on-chain data for LLMs, decentralized compute resources, and autonomous agents that live on the blockchain.
