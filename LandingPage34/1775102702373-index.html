The Artist

A Symphony of Starlight

Based in the pulsing heart of Berlin, Sofia Kline has spent the last decade carving out a unique space in the indie-electronic scene. Her music is a delicate balance of analog warmth and digital precision.

Known for her haunting "dream-core" vocals and cinematic production, Kline’s compositions evoke the quiet moments between the hours, the vastness of the cosmos, and the intimacy of a heartbeat.
