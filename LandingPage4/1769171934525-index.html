Signature Dishes

Charcoal-Grilled Branzino

Whole Mediterranean sea bass, butterfly-cut and grilled over silver oak charcoal. Finished with cold-pressed Cretan olive oil, charred lemon, and sea salt.

Herb-Crusted Coastal Lamb

Grass-fed lamb chops crusted with rosemary and wild oregano, served alongside honey-glazed root vegetables and a cooling mint yogurt reduction.

Azure Heirloom Salad

A celebration of the harvest. Heirloom tomatoes, Persian cucumbers, Kalamata olives, and barrel-aged feta, tossed in a lemon-thyme vinaigrette.
