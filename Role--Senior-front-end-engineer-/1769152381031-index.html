About the conference

The premier virtual event bringing together the brightest minds in AI agents and agentic AI development.

Join us for a deep dive into the next evolution of artificial intelligence. We focus on the practical orchestration, rigorous evaluation, and safe deployment of autonomous agents in real-world enterprise environments.

  • World-class speakers from top AI labs and enterprises.
  • Global community with partners like C# Corner and HackIndia.
  • Cutting-edge insights into multi-agent systems and safety.

What you will learn

Autonomous workflows

Master the design of self-correcting loops and long-running autonomous processes in production.

Multi-agent systems

Explore complex orchestration where specialized agents collaborate to solve high-stakes problems.

LLM orchestration

Deep dive into memory management, context window optimization, and stateful agent interactions.

Production-grade agents

Moving from prototype to scale: latency, reliability, and cost-effective agent deployments.

Evaluation and safety

Frameworks for testing agent behavior, ensuring alignment, and preventing unintended actions.

Tooling and infrastructure

The modern stack for AI agents, including vector databases, monitoring tools, and SDKs.
