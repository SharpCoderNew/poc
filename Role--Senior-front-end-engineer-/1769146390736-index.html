About the conference

The premier virtual event bringing together the brightest minds in AI agents and agentic AI development.

The AI Agents Conference 2026 is a dedicated platform for developers, researchers, and enterprise leaders to dive deep into the next evolution of artificial intelligence. We focus on the shift from passive LLMs to active, autonomous agents capable of complex reasoning, orchestration, and real-world execution.

  • World-class speakers from top AI labs and Fortune 500 companies.
  • Global community reach with partners like C# Corner and HackIndia.
  • Cutting-edge insights on safety, evaluation, and deployment.

What you will learn

Autonomous workflows

Designing agents that can navigate complex tasks with minimal human intervention and high reliability.

Multi-agent systems

Coordinating swarms of specialized agents to solve problems that exceed the capacity of a single model.

LLM orchestration

Advanced techniques for chaining, memory management, and tool-use in modern agentic frameworks.

Production-grade agents

Moving from prototype to production: scaling, observability, and cost-optimization for agentic systems.

Evaluation and safety

Frameworks for testing agent behavior, ensuring alignment, and preventing unintended autonomous actions.

Tooling and infrastructure

The hardware and software stack required to support high-performance, low-latency agentic applications.
